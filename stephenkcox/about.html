Stephen K. Cox

A seasoned aerospace professional with 29 years of experience at NASA, I specialized in payloads, space stations, satellites, and rocket launches. I have directly contributed to the launch of over 180 rockets, holding pivotal roles such as Operations Director, where I provided the critical Go/No-Go system calls. Post-retirement, I am passionate about delivering engaging enrichment talks on science, space, and NASA-related topics, having successfully presented on various cruise lines, including MSC Magnifica, NCL Star, and Sun Princess. My interactive and informative sessions have been well received, showcasing my enthusiasm for inspiring public interest in science and space exploration.

NASA Kennedy Space Center (1991-2018)

Launch System Project (LSP) Operations Manager - Managed Launch Control Center (LCC) operations, communications, and Firing Room systems.

Hangar AE Operations Director - Oversaw over 180 successful rocket launches.

Space Lab Payload Command Data System Lead - Supported over 11 Space Lab missions. Expendable Launch Vehicle (ELV) Avionics Lead. Contributed to the development and integration of ELV systems.

Naval Space Command (1989-1991)

Operations Systems Engineer - Supported Operation Desert Storm in-country.
